⚀⚁⚂⚃⚄⚅   Zhao Nan's  Liar's Dice  ⚅⚄⚃⚂⚁⚀ Every player rolls 5 dice. Players take turns bidding on the number of dice with a certain face value. The next player must either raise the bid or doubt the previous bid.
If the bid is doubted, all dice are revealed and the loser loses a life. The game continues until only one player has lives remaining.
Dice showing a 1 ⚀ are wild and can be used as any face value, but you cannot bid on ones.
Use the arrow keys to change your bid and press "Enter" to submit. Press "d" to doubt.
